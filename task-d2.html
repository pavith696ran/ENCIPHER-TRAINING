KEN TAKAKURA

Ken Takakura, commonly known as "Okarun," is the male protagonist of the Dandadan manga and anime series. He is an introverted high school student obsessed with the occult, specifically aliens and UFOs. After being cursed by the Turbo Granny character, he gains supernatural powers but loses his "kintama" (testes), forcing him to fight spirits and aliens alongside classmate Momo Ayase.
